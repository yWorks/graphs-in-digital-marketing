Digital Marketing Optimization

This is the sample application for the yFiles use case about Graphs in Digital Marketing Optimization.

The app displays a Sankey diagram of the different paths that visitors take on a website - from the page first viewed all the way to a conversion.

With the Threshold, users can filter the network to concentrate on the parts of interest. It specifies a threshold which can best be understood as how many steps or touchpoints the potential customer visited before being converted. A lower threshold means that more paths and touchpoints are shown and, hence, a more complex diagram.

This diagram can be integrated in Power BI, Tableau, or similar dashboard technologies.

This application is powered by yFiles for HTML, the powerful diagramming library.
